About The White Meat Company

Our Story

Founded in Harare, The White Meat Company was born from a bold vision to commercialize rabbit farming and position Zimbabwe as a regional powerhouse in the white meat value chain. By combining local expertise with modern techniques, we provide premium breeding stock, international standard cages and accessories, expert-led Masterclass training and guaranteed markets for our farmers.

500+ satisfied customers
50+ masterclass graduates

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To build a nationwide rabbit farm network and aggregate rabbit meat produce and by-products, into one huge supply quota for mainstream market distribution.

Our Vision

To build a huge rabbit farming industry which will see Zimbabwe become a regional powerhouse and a global player in the rabbit farming value chain.

Essential Rabbit Farming Tips

Housing

Provide adequate space, ventilation, and protection from weather conditions.

Nutrition

Balanced diet with quality pellets, fresh vegetables, and unlimited clean water.

Health Care

Regular health checks, vaccinations, and immediate treatment of any issues.

Records

Keep detailed records of breeding, health, and production for better management.
