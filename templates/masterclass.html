What is the Rabbit Farming Masterclass?

Comprehensive Professional Training

Our Rabbit Farming Masterclass is a comprehensive 2-day intensive program designed to equip you with all the knowledge, skills, and practical experience needed to establish and operate a successful rabbit farming business.

Developed by The White Meat Company with over 7 years of commercial rabbit farming experience, this masterclass combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical training, business planning, and guaranteed market access.

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to scale your existing operation, our masterclass provides the roadmap to profitable rabbit farming in Zimbabwe.

Key Areas Covered in the Masterclass

Setup & Infrastructure

Learn optimal housing design, ventilation systems, and equipment selection for maximum productivity and animal welfare.

Nutrition & Feeding

Master feed formulation, nutritional requirements, and cost-effective feeding strategies for all life stages.

Health Management

Comprehensive health protocols, disease prevention, vaccination schedules, and treatment procedures.

Breeding Programs

Selective breeding techniques, genetic improvement, breeding schedules, and record keeping systems.

Business Management

Financial planning, cost analysis, market strategies, and scaling your operation for maximum profitability.

Market Access

Direct access to our established supply chain and guaranteed market for your premium rabbit meat production.
